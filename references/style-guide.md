# Style Guide (Examples & Inspiration)

> [!TIP]
> 这些是常用的**示例视觉风格**，供设计时作为 moodboard 灵感。生成配图时，**千万不要受限于这些特定模式**。最理想的做法是**自由地、完全根据文章内容设计对应的、定制化的全套视觉语言**（光影、媒介材质如3D/纸艺/摄影、镜头焦段、色调分镜），以达到最高阶的视觉契合度。

## `dimension-breaker`

Use for naming scandals, cultural misunderstandings, and conflict-heavy cover images.

- torn paper
- industrial staging
- hard shadows
- forceful composition

## `titanium-paper`

Use for clean article illustrations and premium editorial inserts.

- off-white paper
- product staging
- soft studio light
- restrained typography

## `blackboard`

Use for explanatory inserts and terminology breakdowns.

- chalk diagrams
- academic annotations
- educational framing

## `retro-cinematic`

Use for historical anecdotes and absurd documentary scenes.

- 1970s-1990s atmosphere
- cinematic still frame
- natural grain
- restrained realism
